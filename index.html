General Tagging

  • amenity=bar, name=Heavy Seas Alehouse
  • Vocabulary is defined on the wiki
  • nodes, ways, and relations
  • nodes: trees, benches, traffic light
  • way: roads, paths
  • way (closed): buildings, area of trees, administrative boundary
  • relation: W&OD is a collection of ways, bus stop + node on road + pole is a relation
  • See also taginfo
  • For bridges, or changes in speed limit, etc. ways connec to ways

Bike Tagging

  • Good bike tagging enables routing
  • Roads are highway=*
  • bicycle=yes for "roads with space"
  • bicycle=designated for paths meant for biking
  • foot=designated for paths meant for walking / running
  • Way A: highway=*, cycleway=lane, "enhance the road"
  • - good when the cycleway is adjacent to the road
  • Or highway=cycleway, cycleway=track
  • - good when the cycleway is not near a road
  • Way A: highway=*, Way B: cycleway=track
  • - good when the cycleway is off-road, e.g. GW Parkway
  • additiona tags: surface=asphalt, one-way=yes
  • sharow: cycleway=shared_lane
  • foot=yes, foot=no
  • user defined tag, cycleway:divider=yes
  • Bicycle Tagging is very well defined
  • But if it gets too confusing see what's happening in the wild
  • See also cycleway on taginfo
